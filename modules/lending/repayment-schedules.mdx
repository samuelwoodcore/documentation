# Repayment Schedules

The Repayment Schedules module manages the generation and management of loan repayment schedules.

## Features

- Schedule Generation
- Schedule Management
- Schedule Updates
- Schedule History
- Schedule Reports
- Schedule Alerts

## Schedule Types

- Equal Installments
- Equal Principal
- Bullet Payment
- Custom Schedule
- Grace Period
- Variable Rate

## Schedule Components

- Principal Amount
- Interest Amount
- Total Payment
- Payment Date
- Balance
- Status

## Schedule Management

- Schedule Creation
- Schedule Modification
- Schedule Updates
- Schedule Tracking
- Schedule History
- Schedule Reports

## Integration

The Repayment Schedules module integrates with:
- Loan Management
- Payment Processing
- Notification Systems
- Reporting Systems
- Analytics Systems
- Collection Systems
