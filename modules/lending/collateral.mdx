# Collateral Management

The Collateral Management module handles the management and tracking of loan collateral.

## Features

- Collateral Registration
- Collateral Valuation
- Collateral Monitoring
- Collateral Release
- Collateral History
- Collateral Reports

## Collateral Types

- Real Estate
- Vehicles
- Financial Assets
- Business Assets
- Personal Assets
- Guarantees

## Collateral Process

1. Collateral Registration
2. Valuation
3. Documentation
4. Monitoring
5. Release
6. History

## Security Measures

- Valuation Verification
- Documentation Checks
- Insurance Requirements
- Risk Assessment
- Compliance Checks
- Audit Trail

## Integration

The Collateral Management module integrates with:
- Loan Management
- Document Management
- Valuation Systems
- Insurance Systems
- Risk Management
- Reporting Systems
