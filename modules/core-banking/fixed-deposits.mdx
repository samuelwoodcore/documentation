# Fixed Deposit Management

The Fixed Deposit Management module specifically handles fixed-term deposit accounts and their operations.

## Features

- Fixed Deposit Account Opening
- Term Management
- Interest Rate Management
- Maturity Processing
- Early Withdrawal
- Auto-renewal

## Fixed Deposit Types

- Standard Fixed Deposits
- Special Rate Fixed Deposits
- Step-up Fixed Deposits
- Callable Fixed Deposits
- Non-callable Fixed Deposits

## Term Management

- Term Selection
- Maturity Date Calculation
- Rollover Options
- Early Closure
- Partial Withdrawal

## Interest Management

- Interest Rate Configuration
- Interest Calculation Methods
- Interest Payment Options
- Tax Deduction
- Interest Statement

## Integration

The Fixed Deposit Management module integrates with:
- Account Management
- Transaction Processing
- Interest Management
- Reporting Systems
- Tax Management
