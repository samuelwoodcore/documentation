# Deposit Management

The Deposit Management module handles all types of deposit accounts and their operations.

## Deposit Types

- Savings Deposits
- Current Deposits
- Term Deposits
- Recurring Deposits
- Special Deposits

## Features

- Deposit Account Opening
- Interest Calculation
- Maturity Management
- Early Withdrawal
- Rollover Processing
- Statement Generation

## Interest Management

- Interest Rate Configuration
- Interest Calculation
- Interest Payment
- Tax Deduction
- Interest Statement

## Operations

- Deposit Placement
- Partial Withdrawal
- Full Withdrawal
- Account Maintenance
- Account Closure

## Integration

The Deposit Management module integrates with:
- Account Management
- Transaction Processing
- Interest Management
- Reporting Systems
- Tax Management
